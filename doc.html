Installation:

Download and Include progress-bar.css & progress-bar.js after including jQuery


<link rel="stylesheet" href="progress-bar.css">
<script src="progress-bar.js"></script>
===================================================

Usage:

Markup Should be like below

<div class="progress-bar">
    <div class="progress" data-percent="60" data-color="green"><span>60%</span></div>
</div>

Use data-percent attribute for counting the percentage and data-color is for progress color

Initialize the animation for progress bar

$(document).ready(function(){
    $(".progress-bar").ProgressBar();
});
